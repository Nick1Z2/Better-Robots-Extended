Version: 1.1.8
Date: 2025-01-29
  Features:
    - New setting "Disables Bobs bots" Disables Bob's bots and Roboports along with their associated research.
    - New setting "Forces a recipe for MK1 bots" Adds an additional recipe for MK1 bots and Roboports based on base-game specifications and places them in the regular category.
    - If "Forces a recipe for MK1 bots" is enabled, a second recipe for logistic-robot, construction-robot, and Roboports is added based on base-game specifications, as the original recipes from Bob's Mods are altered and cannot be easily reverted.
    - If "Forces a recipe for MK1 bots" is enabled, the recipes for MK1 bots and Roboports are unlocked through research.
    - Fixed the order of logistic bots and construction bots, and Roboports so that MK1 appears first, followed by MK2, MK3, MK4, and MK5.
